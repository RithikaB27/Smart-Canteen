About Us

At Smart Canteen, we're passionate about using technology to improve the dining experience for everyone. Our mission is to provide a convenient and healthy dining option that saves time and reduces waste.

Our History

Smart Canteen was founded in 2023 by a group of food enthusiasts and technology experts who wanted to revolutionize the way people dine. We started with a small canteen on a college campus, and quickly grew to serve multiple locations across the city.
